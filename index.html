VCV Rack - DanTModules This is a free plugin I develop for the virtual modular synthesizer application VCV Rack. You can get VCV Rack 2 Free from vcvrack.com and my DanTModules plugin is available direct from the library. If you need help or support with the plugin, you can get it from the DanTModules manual, or from the VCV Community forums.
